## textual-criticism-mcp

An MCP server for biblical text analysis with textual criticism support. Provides morphological analysis, manuscript variants, and concordance searches across Hebrew and Greek biblical texts.

**Features:**
- Full morphological parsing of Hebrew (BHSA) and Greek (N1904) texts via Text-Fabric
- Textual apparatus with manuscript witness data from STEPBible
- Lemma and morphology searches (find all aorist passives, all occurrences of ἀγάπη, etc.)
- Multiple public domain translations (KJV, WEB, ASV, YLT, Darby, BBE)
